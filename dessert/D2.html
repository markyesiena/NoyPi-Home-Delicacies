GINATAANG MAIS

Our ginataang mais is a dessert made from sweet corn kernels cooked in coconut milk and sweetened with sugar. It is a comforting and creamy snack that highlights the natural sweetness of corn and the richness of coconut milk. The coconut milk that we use provides a nutty and aromatic flavor to this snack. This dessert features tender and juicy corn kernels that retain their natural crunch and flavor. Our ginataang mais could be served or eaten either warm or chilled, depending on the special request.
