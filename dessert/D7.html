LECHE FLAN

Our Leche flan is a classic that you'll surely love. This dessert is a sweet and velvety treat that is beloved for its rich flavor and smooth texture. Our leche flan has a silky and smooth texture that melts in your mouth. The star of our leche flan is its rich caramel flavor that is achieved through precise cooking of the caramel. The texture of our leche flan is velvety and luxurious, offering a delightful mouthfeel. As you take a bite, the smooth custard glides across your tongue, providing a creamy and satisfying experience. This dessert is served chilled.
