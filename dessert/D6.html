MANGGO GRAHAM

Our mango graham is a popular "no-bake" dessert that combines the flavors of ripe mangoes, graham crackers, condensed milk, and whipped cream. The combination of condensed milk and whipped cream in our mango graham results in a creamy and luscious texture. hraham crackers are a key component of our mango graham. They provide a pleasant crunch and a slightly nutty flavor to the dessert. This dessert is ordered and served chilled.
