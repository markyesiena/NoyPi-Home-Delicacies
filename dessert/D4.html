SAPIN-SAPIN

Our sapin-sapin is a popular colorful and layered dessert that is usually ordered with the main dishes. Our sapin-sapin is known for its sweet and creamy taste. The combination of coconut milk and sugar that we use gives it a delightful sweetness that is balanced by the richness of the dessert. Also, the glutinous rice flour we used in our sapin-sapin gives it a sticky and chewy texture. Each layer of our sapin-sapin have a different flavor that contribute to the overall taste complexity. Our sapin-sapin is also garnished with latik, which is a mixture of coconut cream and sugar.
