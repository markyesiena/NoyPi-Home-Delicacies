INUTAK

This dessert is a unique and delicious treat made from glutinous rice, coconut milk, sugar, and sometimes purple yam (ube). Our inutak has a rich and creamy texture that is similar to a custard or a sticky rice pudding. This dessert is infused with the distinct flavor of coconut milk. The coconut milk we use is used in both cooking the glutinous rice and in the coconut sauce poured over the top. Our inutak contains grated or pureed purple yam (ube), which contributes a subtle nutty and earthy flavor to the dessert. It is also served with a rich and luscious coconut caramel sauce drizzled over the top. This dessert can be ordered warm or chilled.
