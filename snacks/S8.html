GINATAANG BILO-BILO

Our ginataang bilo-bilo is a famous snack that features sticky rice balls (bilo-bilo) cooked in coconut milk (gata) with various sweet ingredients. The coconut milk we use gives it a velvety texture and imparts a sweet, nutty, and tropical taste. This snack is naturally sweetened with its ingredients including jackfruit (langka), sweet potatoes (kamote), plantains (saba), and tapioca pearls (sago), these ingredients also adds different textures to the snack, creating a delightful contrast. This snack could also be garnished with toasted coconut flakes to enhance its taste.
