DYNAMITE

Our dynamite is a popular snack made from long green chili peppers stuffed with a savory filling and wrapped in spring roll wrapper. This snack is popular to customers thanks to its spicy kick. The long green chili peppers used, provide a fiery heat that adds a bold and exciting element to the dish. The spring roll wrapper we used to encase the chili pepper filling gives this snack its signature crispy and crunchy texture. Cheese is also a star to our dynamite, that is added to the filling, which adds a creamy and melty element. This snack is served with a dipping sauce on the side. This wonderful snack is best enjoyed solo or shared.
