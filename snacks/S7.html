MINI PANCAKES

Our signature mini pancakes, also known as pancake bites, are small, bite-sized versions of your regular pancakes. Our snack have a light and fluffy texture, alongside with a mildly sweet flavor that comes from the addition of sugar. This snack is served with toppings that may include butter, maple syrup, honey, fruit compote, whipped cream, chocolate sauce, or powdered sugar depending on your choice.
