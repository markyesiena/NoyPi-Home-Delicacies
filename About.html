Romel Sano

Founder and CEO
Noypi Home Delicacies was founded by Mr. Romel Sano in 2023 and was established during the same year. It was build to cater services including online food transactions, ordering, delivery, and special requests. It specializes in Filipino home delicacies that ranges from dishes that we all know and love to Filipino desserts, snacks, and beverages. Our business was not established to promote ourselves but solely to bring happiness and satisfaction to our customers and future customers to come.
